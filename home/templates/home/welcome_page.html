Open Build helps small business and non-profits benefit from open source software, and connect with teams of developers.

We recruit, hire and educate software developers from all over the world to use open source software, learn best practices and processes to build better software and build there own agencies and companies to help benefit there local communities.

  • Our partner matching service, we will help you find a developer or development team to build your application.

{% trans "STEM Teaching and Training" %}

With OpenBuild being an active member in the Open Source community, we are deeply passionate about contributing to the spread of learning and knowledge of Open Source technologies, both software and hardware in our local communities.

Our developers, technical specialists, and analysts volunteer their time to work with local organizations and schools to teach STEM based courses.
